About

Vedanta Vidya Peetam is a not-for-profit institution with a goal of delivering hindu religious education and services. An abundance of knowledge is buried in hindu scriptures in the form of slokas and stories. Vedanta Vidya Peetam strives to bring these treasure to the children by way of conducting weekly classes. Vedanta Vidya Peetam is also involved in contributing towards social causes by way of providing donations for orphanages, homeless shelters, public educational institutions, various hunger programs.

Slokas

Why learn slokha? Teaching slokhas to children at an young age helps them in many ways. It develops a tremendous amount of memory to start with. The ability to retain information in the mind for longer time has always been one of the key factors of success. Slokhas provide a structured way to develop that skill. Slokha recitation enhances voice projection, modulation, pronunciation, intonation and clarity. The subtle vibration generated due to the sound energy produced while reciting the slokas causes a soothing sensation in the body and the mind. Slokas are on various gods and this helps the student to know the purpose and importance of various gods in hindu religious practices. Slokha recitation is a form of Yoga that transcendantal elevating the mind to the next level of consciousness.

  • Hanuman chaalisa

Moral Stories

Lots of stories are hidden in puranas, itihaasas and upanishads that teaches great morals and solid values. This class will be a story-telling class that enables children to understand and appreciate the morals in a fun-filled easy way. Currently, the stories class is combined with the Slokhas.
